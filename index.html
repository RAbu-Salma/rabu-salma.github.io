Ph.D. Student at University College London (UCL)

View on GitHub

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at University College London (UCL), under the joint supervision of Prof. M. Angela Sasse and Dr. Sarah Meiklejohn. We belong to the Information Security Research Group and UCL Academic Center of Excellence for Cyber Security Research. Broadly, I study user-centered and secure messaging. My research is funded by a Google Research Award.

I earned an M.Sc. (with Distinction) in Information Security from UCL in 2014. My dissertation was on Differentially Private Statistics. Before joining UCL, I obtained a B.Sc. (1st Hons) in Computer Engineering from the American University of Sharjah.

Outside of academia, I aspire to become a professional photographer. I have taken several photography courses at City Academy in the UK. I am also an active member of the Photography Society at UCL. Personally, I <3 language learning and travelling.

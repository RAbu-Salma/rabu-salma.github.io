I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at University College London (UCL), under the joint supervision of Prof. M. Angela Sasse and Dr. Sarah Meiklejohn. We belong to the Information Security Research Group and UCL Academic Center of Excellence for Cyber Security Research. Broadly, I research user-centered and secure messaging. I aim to understand how users perceive secure messaging, and compare that with the actual underlying security of different messaging tools.

My research is funded by a Google Research Award, supporting "open-source secure messaging development research".

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at University College London (UCL), under the joint supervision of Prof. M. Angela Sasse and Dr. Sarah Meiklejohn. We belong to the Information Security Research Group and UCL Academic Center of Excellence for Cyber Security Research. Broadly, I research user-centered and secure messaging. My research is funded by a Google Research Award.

I earned an M.Sc. (with Distinction) in Information Security from UCL in 2014, and a B.Sc. (1st Hons) in Computer Engineering from the American University of Sharjah (AUS) in 2013.

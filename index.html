I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at University College London (UCL), supervised by Prof. M. Angela Sasse and Dr. Sarah Meiklejohn. We belong to the Information Security Research Group. Broadly, I study user-centered and secure communications. I am supported by a SUDS Fellowship from the Open Technology Fund and Simply Secure. My research is also funded by Google.

I earned an M.Sc. (with Distinction) in Information Security from UCL in 2014. Before joining UCL, I obtained a B.Sc. (1st Hons) in Computer Engineering from the American University of Sharjah in 2013.

Outside of academia, I aspire to become a professional photographer. I have taken several photography courses at City Academy in the UK. I am also a member of the Photography Society at UCL. Personally, I <3 learning new languages and travelling.

Find me on Twitter: Ruba_AbuSalma.
